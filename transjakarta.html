Transjakarta Analysis
Stop Density Analysis

This foundational heatmap visualizes the concentration of Transjakarta stops. Brighter, "hotter" areas indicate a higher density of stops, which generally corresponds to zones with greater transit accessibility and service availability.

Route Speed Analysis

This map displays the average operational speed of different bus routes. The color scale (Yellow = Slower, Red = Faster) helps quickly identify routes that may be impacted by traffic congestion versus those that operate more efficiently.

Network Communities Analysis

This analysis reveals the underlying structure of the network by identifying distinct clusters (or "communities") of closely connected stops. The algorithm found 752 communities, visualized by different colors.

It also identifies critical "bridge routes" that connect these communities. Top examples include:

  • Route 3: Kalideres - Monas
  • JAK.108: Kramat Jati - Bambu Apus
Network Centrality Analysis

This map identifies the most important and influential stops in the network based on their position and connections.

Bridges (Blue): Stops crucial for connecting different parts of the network. Top bridges include Potlot and Simpang Danau Sunter Selatan.

Hubs (Orange): Stops with the most direct connections. Key hubs are Jln. Taman Jatibaru Timur and Cibubur Junction.

Bivariate Transit Gap Analysis

This capstone analysis compares transit service levels (proximity to stops) with population density to identify service gaps.

The color scheme is key: Red indicates low service, while Blue indicates high service. Brightness corresponds to population density.
