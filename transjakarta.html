Stop Density Route Speed Communities Centrality Gap Analysis

Stop Density Analysis: This heatmap shows stop concentrations. Brighter areas mean higher stop density and better transit access.

Route Speed Analysis: Displays average route speeds. Yellow = Slower, Red = Faster. This helps identify traffic congestion points.

Network Communities Analysis: Reveals the network's structure by identifying 752 distinct clusters (communities) of connected stops. It also highlights "bridge routes" that link these communities, like Route 3 (Kalideres - Monas).

Network Centrality Analysis: Identifies influential stops. Bridges (Blue), like Simpang Danau Sunter Selatan, connect network parts. Hubs (Orange), like Cibubur Junction, have the most direct connections.

Bivariate Transit Gap Analysis: This compares service with population. Red = Low Service, Blue = High Service. Brightness shows population. Bright red areas are critical service gaps (high population, poor transit) for prioritization.
